HAPO Visa Cards Perks:

Instant Issue Cards

At HAPO, meeting your needs in an instant is important to us. That is why we offer instant issue debit and credit cards. Visit any of our financial centers during regular business hours and we can print a new card for you on the spot—instantly! Best of all, it is another service HAPO offers you for FREE!*

Contact HAPO for details and exclusions that may apply.

*Initial card prints at account opening are free. Replacement cards are assessed a $5.00 fee upon printing.

Visa eStatements: Experience the ultimate in convenience

Receiving your statement electronically is faster, more secure, and more environmentally friendly than receiving paper statements. Enroll today!

  1. Log in to your account in HAPO's Online Banking
  2. Click Card Management, then Card Services
  3. Select your card
  4. Click Statements
  5. Click eStatement Enrollment

Member Alerts

Choose to receive text or email notices when your credit card is used and know what is happening on your account all the time! To receive notifications, go into Online Banking, click on Card Managment, then Card Services and select the card you would like notifications on, then click Notification Settings.

Fraud Monitoring

HAPO's fraud monitoring team detects and notifies you of any unusual card activity to help prevent fraud. Block or unblock your card in real time. From Online Banking, click on Card Management, Card Services, and Click the Lock Card icon. 

Zero Liability

Use your Visa card to shop online, in a store, or anywhere, and you're protected from unauthorized use of your card or account information. With Visa's Zero Liability policy, your liability for unauthorized transactions is $0-you pay nothing!

Lost/Stolen Card Reporting

Enjoy the comfort in knowing that if your Visa card is lost or stolen, assistance is only a phone call away through Visa's Lost/Stolen Card Reporting service. Call 800-820-6805 anytime, 24 hours a day to report theft or fraud.

Emergency Cash & Card

Experience peace of mind 24 hours a day, seven days a week knowing that Visa has you covered through Visa Emergency Cash & Card.

Debt Protection** 

We have you covered!

  • Pays off or reduces your loan balance in the event of death
  • Pays your loan payment for up to 6 months if you are disabled (begins 30-days after disability)
  • Defers your loan payment up to 3 months if you become unemployed
  • Defers your loan payment up to 3 months for Family Medical Leave
  • Easily added to your current loan
  • Instant coverage 90-days after signing your loan
  • Joint protection available. You choose the coverage that is right for you!

**Must meet all Debt Protection Plan Agreement terms to qualify.
